隐私政策

最后更新时间： 2026年3月

Task Flyout（以下简称“本软件”）深知个人隐私的重要性。我们的核心理念是：您的数据，只属于您。

1. 我们收集什么数据？

本软件不收集、不存储、不上传您的任何个人敏感信息至任何第三方服务器。 本软件仅作为连接您设备与官方日历服务（Google API、Microsoft Graph API）的本地桥梁。

2. OAuth 授权与数据使用

当您授权本软件访问您的 Google 或 Microsoft 账户时：

  • 您的访问令牌（Token）仅通过系统级加密保存在您本地计算机的硬盘上。
  • 您的日程和任务数据仅在您的设备内存中进行加载和展示，关闭软件后即从内存释放。
  • 本软件遵守 Google API 服务用户数据政策，仅请求实现日历读写功能所必需的最少权限。

3. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或安全担忧，请通过 GitHub Issues 联系开发者。

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Task Flyout ("we", "our", or "the App") understands the importance of personal privacy. Our core philosophy is simple: Your data belongs to you.

1. What data do we collect?

The App DOES NOT collect, store, or upload any of your personal or sensitive information to any third-party servers. It functions strictly as a local bridge connecting your device to official calendar services (Google API, Microsoft Graph API).

2. OAuth Authorization and Data Usage

When you authorize the App to access your Google or Microsoft accounts:

  • Your access tokens are encrypted and stored locally on your computer's hard drive.
  • Your schedule and task data are loaded solely into your device's memory for display purposes and are released once the app is closed.
  • The App complies fully with the Google API Services User Data Policy, requesting only the minimum scopes necessary to provide calendar and task functionalities.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions or security concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via GitHub Issues.
